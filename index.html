THE RESEARCHER

The deeper I explored AI, the more I became interested in systems that understand context across time—not just isolated inputs. That curiosity led me into healthcare AI. I worked on clinical intelligence systems capable of longitudinal reasoning across radiology reports, where the challenge isn't just extraction—it's memory. A "nodule" becomes a "lesion."
